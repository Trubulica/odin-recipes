Stuffed Peppers

Recipe

My favorite meal!

Easy to make, tastes wonderful. Add a spoon of sour cream for an even stronger taste!

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Minced meat
  • Rice
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Chop onions
  2. Fry onions, meat and rice
  3. Stuff peppers with onions, meat and rice
  4. Cook them
